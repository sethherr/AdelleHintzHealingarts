Adelle Hintz

CMT #53791 CA | LMT #18194 OR

I began my study of massage and bodywork in 2009, in Portland Oregon, graduating from East West College of the Healing Arts to become a professional massage therapist. I moved to Oakland California in 2013, in search of blue skies and sunshine. I am an avid cyclist with a background in martial arts and yoga. I am passionate about creating a supportive community by facilitating wellness through therapeutic human touch and I am committed to an open and inviting atmosphere using honest and constructive communication.

I specialize in Therapeutic Massage, Sports Massage and Deep Relaxation, using my combined training in Western and Eastern modalities that include Acupressure, Deep Tissue, and Swedish Massage techniques.

I offer a safe space to all genders and identities including religious and ethnic. And am committed to using only cruelty free products.
